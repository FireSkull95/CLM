Technical Parameters

T-18 sheet metal is always relevant and stylish, and suits to buildings of different configurations.

With its classic shape, T-18 is successfully used for restoration works, and facade replacement of residential and industrial buildings, as well as in the construction of garages, fences and gates.

T-18 also features a polymeric coating, which is highly durable and able to resist corrosion in even the harshest environments.
