Customers of CLM Steel Roofing can also purchase all the necessary installation hardware, to ensure its correct installation.

All items are made from galvanized steel with polymer coating. Accessories and additional material include: skate strips, windscreen strips, sill protection strips, curtain strips, snow catchers, etc. Custom order manufacturing is available.



Sealers

The main function of these sealers is to protect the structures from inclimate weather, while still allowing the passage of air, importantly enabling the roof to "breathe".



Screws

Specialized self-fastening screws are used for sheet metal and metal shingles, which recently completely replaced the use of nails. The screws also feature a sealing gasket, which not only provides water proofing, but also a stronger fastening.
