THE LIFESPAN OF METAL ROOFS GREATLY DEPENDS ON THE SERVICE LIFE OF ALL COMPONENTS

Customers of CLM Steel Roofing have no need to buy additional hardware components for the roof and its correct installation. The CLM Steel Roofing company provides the full set of required elements needed for correct installation and durable service of the materials.

All items are made from galvanized steel with polymer coating. Accessories and additional material include: skate strips, windscreen strips, sill protection strips, curtain strips, snow catchers, etc. Custom order manufacturing is possible.



Sealers

The main function of seals is the protection of the internal space from snow, dust and allowing the air to pass (withdrawal of vapor from roof) which allows the roof to "breathe".



Screws

As fastening elements for sheet metal and metal singles screws with self-fastening ability are used, which recently completely replaced the use of nails. Main advantage of using screws with sealing gasket is its full water resistance and high attachment force. Beside that, screws also greatly reduce difficulty of installation and and replacement process.
