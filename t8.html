Technical Parameters

T-8 Metal Sheeting is made of galvanized steel with polymer coating that is both durable and easy to install. Most commonly for exterior use on structures, T-8 Sheet metal is actually one of the most popular exterior façade materials on the market.

T-8 Sheet metal is widely used for both new constructions as well as for replacing facades on existing structures. T-8 is available in a variety of colours and comes in both matte and glossy finishes.

T-8 Profile is also widely used as a material to construct storage and industrial outbuildings.
