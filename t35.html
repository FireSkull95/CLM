Technical Parameters

T-35 sheet metal is a common solution for roofing commercial facilities, warehouses and other industrial facilities with a large roof area.

Available in a variety of colours and finishes from European Steel manufacturers, CLM's T-35 is a popular choice among builders.

T-35 features a polymeric coating, which is highly durable and able to resist corrosion in aggressive environments
